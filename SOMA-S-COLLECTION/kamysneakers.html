How To Order

01

02

Use our True-To-Size guide. If you're between sizes, we recommend going half a size up.

04

Shoe Care

Keep your kicks looking fresh with these care tips from our product team.

🧹

Clean After Every Wear

Wipe down the sole and upper with a damp cloth after each use to prevent dirt buildup.

🌲

Air Dry Only

Never put your sneakers in the dryer. Stuff them with newspaper and leave in a ventilated area.

💕

Use a Sneaker Protector

Spray water and stain repellent on new shoes before first wear. Re-apply every 3–4 weeks.

📦

Store in the Box

Keep shoes in their original box away from direct sunlight to preserve color and material integrity.
